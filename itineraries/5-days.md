# Naxos 5-Day Itinerary — The Recommended First Visit

Five nights is the minimum to experience the full range of Naxos: beach coast, mountain interior, villages, and archaeology without rushing.

---

## Day 1 — Arrive and settle into Chora

- Arrive by ferry or flight → private transfer: [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)
- Walk the old town: Portara, Kastro, the old market lanes
- Dinner in Chora waterfront

---

## Day 2 — West coast beaches

- Full beach day at Agios Prokopios and Agia Anna
- Walk the sand strip south to Plaka in the afternoon
- Sunset drinks at a beach bar

---

## Day 3 — Mountain villages full-day private tour

The highlight of any Naxos visit. Book a full-day private tour (6 hours):

**Itinerary:**
- Sangri — Temple of Demeter (530 BC)
- Halki — Venetian tower, kitron distillery, Byzantine church
- Filoti — largest mountain village, lunch at a traditional taverna
- Apeiranthos — marble-paved streets, small archaeological museum
- Viewpoints over the Aegean on the return

**Book:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours)

---

## Day 4 — North coast and Apollonas

Hire a car or book a private tour to the north:

- Koronos village — remote, off the tourist trail
- Apollonas — fishing village with the famous 8th-century BC Kouros statue (10.5 m, unfinished, lying in the ancient quarry)
- North coast road with sea views
- Return along the east coast

---

## Day 5 — Relaxed beach morning, depart

- Final morning at Plaka or Agia Anna
- Lunch in Chora
- Private transfer to port or airport: [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

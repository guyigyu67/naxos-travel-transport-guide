# Naxos 3-Day Itinerary — First-Timer Plan

Three days is enough to see Chora, the best west-coast beaches, and one mountain village. Here is how to use the time well.

---

## Day 1 — Arrive, explore Chora, first beach

**Morning: arrival**
- Ferry or flight arrives → private transfer to your hotel: [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)
- Drop bags, walk the port waterfront

**Late morning: Chora**
- Portara (Temple of Apollo gate) — 10 min walk from port, free, open any time
- Kastro (Venetian castle) — marble-lane old town, 13th-century walls
- Explore the Bourgos neighbourhood below the castle

**Afternoon: first beach**
- Agios Georgios — walking distance from Chora, shallow, family-friendly
- Or take a 15-min transfer to Agios Prokopios for longer sand

**Evening**
- Dinner in Chora — try graviera cheese, fresh fish, local wine
- Sunset from the Portara if you haven't already

---

## Day 2 — Beach day on the west coast

**Morning**
- Private transfer or bus to Agios Prokopios or Agia Anna
- Sunbeds, swimming, water sports

**Afternoon**
- Walk south from Agia Anna to Plaka (30 min, flat sand)
- Lunch at a beach taverna in Plaka or Maragas

**Evening**
- Transfer back to Chora: [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)
- Evening meal in Chora old town

---

## Day 3 — Mountain village half-day tour, then depart

**Morning: private half-day tour**
Book a 4-hour private tour covering one or two inland highlights before your ferry or flight.

Recommended stops:
- Halki — Byzantine churches, Vallindras kitron distillery (1896), neoclassical mansions
- Temple of Demeter at Sangri (530 BC, restored Naxian marble temple)
- Viewpoint over the Tragea olive plain

**Book:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours)

**Afternoon: departure**
- Transfer to port or airport: [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

# Naxos 7-Day Itinerary — The Full Island Experience

Seven nights is the sweet spot for Naxos. Enough time to explore the entire island without rushing, with proper rest days built in.

---

## Day 1 — Arrive

- Ferry or flight → private transfer to hotel: [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)
- Walk the Chora waterfront, find a taverna, early night

---

## Day 2 — Chora deep dive

- Morning: Kastro (Venetian old town), Archaeological Museum of Naxos
- Portara at golden hour
- Evening: dinner in the Bourgos quarter

---

## Day 3 — West beaches

- Agios Prokopios → Agia Anna → Plaka (walk the full 6 km beach strip)
- Lunch and sunbeds at Maragas
- Return transfer: [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

---

## Day 4 — Central villages full-day private tour

- Sangri (Temple of Demeter)
- Halki (kitron distillery, Byzantine churches)
- Filoti (lunch, views of Mount Zas)
- Apeiranthos (marble village, museums)

**Book:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours)

---

## Day 5 — South coast and cedar forest

- Alyko beach — cedar and juniper forest meeting white sand, no sunbeds
- Hawaii beach (unofficial name) — secluded cove within the cedar forest
- Kalantos — remote south-coast bay
- Private driver by the hour recommended for flexibility: [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

---

## Day 6 — North coast: Apollonas and Koronos

- Drive or private tour up the east coast road
- Koronos — traditional village, mountain taverna
- Apollonas — Kouros statue (8th century BC, 10.5 m), fishing harbour, fish lunch
- Return along the north coast

---

## Day 7 — Final morning, depart

- Last swim at your favourite beach
- Chora souvenir shopping: local cheese (graviera, arseniko), Naxian thyme honey, kitron liqueur
- Private transfer to port or airport: [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

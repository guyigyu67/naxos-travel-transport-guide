# Apeiranthos, Naxos

Apeiranthos (also spelled Apiranthos) is a marble-paved mountain village 30 km east of Chora, considered one of the most beautiful villages in the Cyclades.

## Getting there

| From | Distance | Transfer time | Approx. fare |
|---|---|---|---|
| Naxos Port | 30 km | 35 min | €32–38 |
| Chora | 30 km | 35 min | €30–35 |

**Book a private transfer or tour:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours)

## About the village

- Streets, steps, and walls entirely paved in white marble
- Four small museums: Archaeological, Natural History, Visual Arts, Folklore
- Cretan-influenced dialect and architecture (descendants of 17th-century settlers)
- Excellent views toward the Aegean

## As part of a private tour

Apeiranthos is the highlight stop on full-day [Naxos private tours](../private-tours.md). Allow at least 1.5 hours to walk the village and visit a museum.

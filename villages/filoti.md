# Filoti, Naxos

Filoti is the largest village in the Naxos interior, 18 km east of Chora on the slopes of Mount Zas (the highest peak in the Cyclades, 1,001 m).

## Getting there

| From | Distance | Transfer time | Approx. fare |
|---|---|---|---|
| Naxos Port | 18 km | 25 min | €22–27 |
| Chora | 18 km | 25 min | €22–27 |

**Book a private transfer or tour:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours)

## About the village

- Traditional Cycladic village with a central plateia (square)
- Tavernas serving local meat dishes and Naxian wine
- Starting point for the hike up Mount Zas
- Tower of Chimara visible nearby (Venetian-era fortified tower)

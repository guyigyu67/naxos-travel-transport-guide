# Agios Prokopios, Naxos

Agios Prokopios is Naxos's most popular beach area, 8 km southwest of Chora.

## Getting there

| From | Distance | Transfer time | Approx. fare |
|---|---|---|---|
| Naxos Port | 8 km | 12 min | €15–18 |
| Naxos Airport | 6 km | 10 min | €14–17 |
| Chora | 8 km | 12 min | €15–18 |

**Book a private transfer:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com/naxos-port-to-agios-prokopios-transfer](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

## About the area

- Long sandy beach with shallow water, ideal for families
- Wide range of hotels, studios, and villas
- Water sports, beach bars, tavernas along the waterfront
- Connects south to Agia Anna beach (walkable)

## Nearby

- Agia Anna beach — 1.5 km south
- Plaka beach — 5 km south
- Chora (Naxos Town) — 8 km north

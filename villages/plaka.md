# Plaka Beach, Naxos

Plaka is a long, undeveloped sandy beach 13 km southwest of Chora. Less crowded than Agios Prokopios.

## Getting there

| From | Distance | Transfer time | Approx. fare |
|---|---|---|---|
| Naxos Port | 13 km | 18 min | €20–25 |
| Naxos Airport | 11 km | 15 min | €18–22 |

**Book a private transfer:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

## About the area

- 4 km of uninterrupted sandy beach
- Cedar trees behind the beach provide shade
- Limited facilities — quieter, more natural feel
- Nudism tolerated at southern end

## Nearby

- Agia Anna — 3 km north
- Agios Prokopios — 5 km north
- Kastraki — 2 km south

# Naxos Airport Transfer Guide

Everything you need to know about getting from Naxos National Airport (JNX) to your hotel, villa, or beach.

---

## Naxos Airport (JNX) basics

- **IATA code:** JNX
- **Location:** 3 km south of Naxos Chora
- **Connections:** Athens (ATH) year-round; seasonal routes from other Greek airports
- **Terminal:** Single terminal, small and straightforward

---

## Getting from the airport into Naxos

### Option 1 — Pre-booked private transfer (recommended)

A private transfer meets you at arrivals with a name board, no waiting, fixed price. The driver monitors your flight in real time and adjusts for delays.

**Book:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com/naxos-airport-transfer](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com/naxos-airport-transfer)

Typical fares:
| Destination | Fixed price |
|---|---|
| Chora | from €12 |
| Agios Prokopios | from €18 |
| Plaka | from €22 |
| Filoti | from €28 |

### Option 2 — Metered taxi

Taxis wait outside the terminal when flights arrive, but supply is limited. During summer peak season (July–August) there can be a queue.

### Option 3 — Bus (KTEL)

A bus connects the airport to Chora bus terminal. Frequency is low and it does not serve beach villages directly.

---

## Tips

- **Late-night flights:** Pre-book a transfer. Taxi availability drops sharply after 22:00.
- **Heavy luggage:** Confirm vehicle capacity when booking if you have more than 2 large bags.
- **Group travel:** A private minivan transfer is often cheaper per person than multiple taxis.

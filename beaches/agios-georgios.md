# Agios Georgios Beach, Naxos

Agios Georgios is the closest beach to Naxos Town (Chora), about 1.5 km from the port. Walking distance from most Chora hotels.

## Character

- Long sandy beach curving along the bay south of Chora
- Very shallow water — ideal for young children and non-swimmers
- Windsurfing and kitesurfing school at the far end (the flat lagoon is one of Greece's best learning spots)
- Beach bars, sunbed rentals, tavernas along the seafront

## Getting there

- **On foot** from Chora: 15–20 min walk along the waterfront promenade
- **Taxi** from the port: €5–8

## Nearby

- Chora old town and Kastro — 1.5 km
- Agios Prokopios — 6 km south

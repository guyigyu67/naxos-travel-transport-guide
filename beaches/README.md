# Naxos Beaches — Complete Guide (2026)

Naxos has over 20 beaches, most on the west and south coasts. This guide covers every beach worth visiting, with transfer info for each.

---

## West coast (main strip)

These beaches are connected by a continuous sand strip and are served by the KTEL bus from Chora.

| Beach | Distance from port | Character | Private transfer |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Agios Georgios](agios-georgios.md) | 1.5 km | Town beach, shallow, family | Walk or short taxi |
| [Agios Prokopios](agios-prokopios.md) | 8 km | Most popular, full facilities | [Book transfer](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com) |
| [Agia Anna](agia-anna.md) | 10 km | Cosmopolitan, beach bars | [Book transfer](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com) |
| [Plaka](plaka.md) | 13 km | Long, undeveloped, bohemian | [Book transfer](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com) |
| Maragas | 14 km | Quiet extension of Plaka | [Book transfer](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com) |
| Kastraki | 15 km | Windsurfing, kite surfing | [Book transfer](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com) |
| [Mikri Vigla](mikri-vigla.md) | 18 km | Windsurfing world-class spot | [Book transfer](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com) |

---

## South coast

More secluded. No bus service — private transfer or rental car required.

| Beach | Distance from port | Character |
|---|---|---|
| Aliko | 21 km | Cedar forest, no sunbeds, natural |
| Hawaii | 22 km | Hidden cove within cedar forest |
| Pyrgaki | 24 km | Remote, clear water |
| Kalantos | 28 km | Sheltered bay, very quiet |
| Psili Ammos | 32 km | Fine white sand, turquoise water |

**Book a private transfer to south coast beaches:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

## East coast

Rocky and windswept. Worth visiting as part of a north coast tour.

| Beach | Distance from port | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Moutsouna | 32 km | Former emery port, fishing village |
| Lionas | 40 km | Small pebble beach, crystal clear |

---

## Best beach by type

| You want | Go to |
|---|---|
| Families with small children | Agios Georgios, Agios Prokopios |
| Long walk on empty sand | Plaka, Maragas |
| Beach bars and nightlife | Agia Anna |
| Windsurfing / kite surfing | Mikri Vigla, Kastraki |
| Total solitude | Aliko, Psili Ammos, Kalantos |
| Shallow flat water | Agios Georgios, Agios Prokopios lagoon |

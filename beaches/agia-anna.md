# Agia Anna Beach, Naxos

Agia Anna is a lively beach resort 10 km from Chora, connected to Agios Prokopios to the north and Plaka to the south.

## Character

- Popular with couples and younger travellers
- Cosmopolitan vibe, beach bars, good tavernas
- Sand is fine and golden, water deep enough for swimming immediately
- Small port with local fishing boats at the north end

## Getting there

| From | Method | Journey time | Approx. fare |
|---|---|---|---|
| Naxos Port | Private transfer | 15 min | €17–20 |
| Naxos Airport | Private transfer | 12 min | €15–18 |
| Chora | KTEL bus | 20 min | €2 |

**Book a private transfer:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

## Walking connections

Agia Anna is part of a continuous beach strip:
- North to Agios Prokopios: 2 km flat sand walk
- South to Plaka: 3 km flat sand walk (the full strip is one of Greece's longest unbroken sandy beaches)

## Nearby

- Agios Prokopios — 2 km north
- Plaka — 3 km south
- Chora — 10 km

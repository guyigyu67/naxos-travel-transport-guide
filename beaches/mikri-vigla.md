# Mikri Vigla Beach, Naxos

Mikri Vigla is a world-class windsurfing and kitesurfing destination 18 km from Chora, consistently rated one of the best wind sports beaches in Europe.

## Character

- The beach splits into two sides: the north side (sheltered, good swimming) and the south side (exposed to the Meltemi, for wind sports)
- Meltemi wind (July–August) regularly reaches Force 5–6 — ideal for experienced windsurfers and kiters
- Lessons and equipment rental available at beach centres
- Quieter than the main Agios Prokopios strip

## Getting there

No bus service. Private transfer or rental car required.

| From | Journey time | Approx. fare |
|---|---|---|
| Naxos Port | 25 min | €22–28 |
| Naxos Airport | 20 min | €20–25 |

**Book a private transfer:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

## Tips

- Go early in July–August: the wind picks up by midday and can be very strong
- The south-facing side is not suitable for children when the Meltemi is up
- Combine with Kastraki beach (2 km north) for a full day

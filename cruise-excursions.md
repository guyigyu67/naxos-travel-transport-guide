## Port day logistics

Cruise ships anchor offshore at Naxos and tender passengers to the port in Chora. Most port days run 8–12 hours. The port is in the centre of Chora — the Portara is visible from the ship.

**Typical time ashore:** 7–9 hours depending on itinerary.

## Recommended itineraries for a port day

### 4-hour shore excursion (half-day)

- Portara and Chora old town (walk from port)
- Halki village — kitron distillery tasting
- Temple of Demeter at Sangri
- Return to port

### 6-hour shore excursion (most popular)

- Chora old town and Kastro
- Sangri — Temple of Demeter
- Halki — Vallindras distillery
- Filoti — mountain village coffee stop
- Apeiranthos — marble-paved streets, quick museum visit
- Return to port with time to spare

### 8-hour full-island excursion

- All of the above, plus:
- Apollonas — Kouros statue, fish lunch at waterfront taverna
- North coast scenic drive
- Return along east coast

## What to see near the port (on foot, no transfer needed)

- **Portara** — 10-min walk, free, the iconic marble gateway
- **Kastro** — 15-min walk uphill, the Venetian old town
- **Chora waterfront** — cafes, tavernas, shops along the port road
- **Archaeological Museum of Naxos** — inside the Kastro, excellent Cycladic collection

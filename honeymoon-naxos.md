# Naxos Honeymoon & Romantic Getaway Guide (2026)

Naxos offers a more authentic and less crowded alternative to Santorini and Mykonos for couples and honeymooners — with better food, longer beaches, and a quieter pace.

---

## Why Naxos for a honeymoon?

- **Less crowded** than Santorini and Mykonos — easier to find private moments
- **Better food** — the most fertile island in the Cyclades, excellent local cuisine
- **Longer beaches** — Plaka's 4 km of sand is one of the longest in the Aegean
- **Authentic character** — marble villages, Byzantine churches, olive groves
- **Comparable luxury** — high-end villas and boutique hotels at lower prices than neighbouring islands

## Romantic experiences

### Sunset at the Portara

The Portara (Temple of Apollo gate) frames the sun setting over Paros — one of the most photographed sunsets in Greece. Free, 10 minutes from the port, no crowds compared to Santorini's caldera.

### Private tour of the mountain villages

A private car tour for two through Halki, Filoti, and Apeiranthos with a taverna lunch and kitron tasting at the Vallindras distillery (1896).

### Cedar forest beaches

Alyko and Hawaii beaches are set within a protected cedar and juniper forest — wild, undeveloped, and quiet even in summer. Reachable by private transfer or rental car.

**Private transfer to Alyko:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

### Dinner in Apeiranthos

A taverna dinner in the marble-paved mountain village of Apeiranthos at golden hour — local lamb, Naxian wine, views over the Aegean.

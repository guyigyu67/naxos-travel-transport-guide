# Naxos Taxi Prices — 2025/2026

Complete fare guide for taxis and private transfers on Naxos Island, Greece.

---

## How Naxos taxis work

Naxos taxis run on a meter with two tariffs:
- **Tariff 1** — within Chora / town limits
- **Tariff 2** — outside town (most beach and village routes)

Night surcharge applies from 24:00 to 05:00. Luggage over 10 kg may incur a small addition.

## Private transfer vs metered taxi

| | Metered taxi | Private transfer |
|---|---|---|
| Pricing | Metered, variable | Fixed price, agreed in advance |
| Booking | On the spot | Pre-booked |
| Flight/ferry tracking | No | Yes — driver adjusts for delays |
| Vehicle | Standard | Mercedes-Benz, air-conditioned |
| English | Varies | Guaranteed |
| Night availability | Limited | On request |

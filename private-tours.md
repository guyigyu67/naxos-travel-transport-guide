# Naxos Private Tours — Full Guide

Everything you need to know about booking a private island tour on Naxos, Greece.

---

## Why a private tour?

Naxos has one of the most varied interiors of any Cyclades island — marble quarries, Byzantine towers, mountain villages, ancient temples, and nearly 40 km of beaches. Public buses only reach the main beach strip. A private car tour with a local driver covers the entire island at your own pace.

---

## Tour formats

### Half-day private tour (4 hours)

Best for: first-time visitors, cruise passengers, short stays.

Typical itinerary:
- Naxos Chora old town and castle (Kastro)
- Portara (Temple of Apollo gate)
- One or two west-coast beaches
- Village coffee stop

**Book:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours)

---

### Full-day private tour (6 hours)

Best for: travellers wanting to see the mountain interior.

Typical itinerary:
- Halki — neoclassical village, Byzantine church, citron distillery
- Filoti — largest mountain village, views of Mount Zas
- Apeiranthos — marble-paved streets, small museums
- Panoramic viewpoints

**Book:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours)

---

### Full-day private tour with lunch (8 hours)

Best for: those who want a relaxed, complete island experience.

Adds a taverna lunch in a mountain village to the full-day itinerary. Local cuisine: lamb, fresh cheese (graviera, arseniko), local wine.

---

### Custom private tour

Design your own itinerary. Popular additions:
- Koronos village and the north coast
- Apollonas (giant kouros statue)
- Naxos beaches: Plaka, Kalantos, Psili Ammos
- Sunset at the Portara

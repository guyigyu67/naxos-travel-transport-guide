# Day Trip to Naxos from Mykonos, Santorini or Paros (2026)

Naxos is one of the best day trips from the neighbouring Cyclades islands. Here is how to plan it.

---

## Ferry times from nearby islands

| From | Ferry type | Duration | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mykonos | High-speed | 45 min | Multiple daily |
| Santorini | High-speed | 2h | Several daily |
| Paros | High-speed | 25–35 min | Multiple daily |
| Ios | High-speed | 1h | Several daily |

Book ferry tickets at ferryhopper.com. In July–August, book at least a week ahead.

---

## How much time do you have?

A day trip from Paros or Mykonos gives you 6–8 hours on Naxos (depending on ferry times). From Santorini, you might have 5–6 hours.

---

## Best use of a day trip to Naxos

### Option A — Beach day (easiest)

Take the ferry → private transfer to Agios Prokopios or Plaka → full beach day → transfer back to port → ferry home.

**Transfer both ways:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

### Option B — Villages + lunch (most popular for day trippers)

Arrive at port → private half-day or full-day tour → return to port.

Covers: Portara, Chora old town, Halki, Temple of Demeter, Filoti or Apeiranthos. Includes a traditional taverna lunch in the mountains.

**Book a day-trip private tour:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com/private-tours)

### Option C — Mix: one village + one beach

- Morning: private transfer to Halki (45 min each way, 1h in village)
- Afternoon: transfer to Agios Prokopios for a few hours
- Evening: transfer back to port

---

## Why Naxos is worth a day trip

Naxos is the largest, greenest, and most self-sufficient Cycladic island — a sharp contrast to the party scene of Mykonos or the caldera views of Santorini. The food is better, the beaches longer, and the interior has a completely different character from anything else in the Cyclades.

Most visitors who do Naxos as a day trip wish they had stayed longer.

---

## Practical tips

- The port is central — no transfer needed to reach Chora on foot
- Book your return ferry before you leave: last high-speed boats back to Mykonos/Santorini are often around 17:00–18:00
- Pre-book your private tour so you maximise time and don't lose hours figuring out taxis at the port

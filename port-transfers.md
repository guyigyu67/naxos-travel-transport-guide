# Naxos Port Transfer Guide

Arriving by ferry to Naxos? Here is everything you need for a smooth transfer from the port.

---

## Naxos Port (Chora)

The main port of Naxos is in Chora (Naxos Town). Ferries arrive from:
- Piraeus (Athens) — multiple daily sailings
- Mykonos
- Santorini (Thira)
- Paros
- Ios and other Cyclades islands

Ferry schedules are subject to weather delays and last-minute changes, especially with Meltemi winds in July and August.

### Metered taxi

Taxis queue at the port exit. During busy summer arrivals (Blue Star Ferries often bring 300+ passengers) the queue can be long.

### Walking

Chora centre is 5–10 minutes on foot from the port. If your hotel is in town, you may not need any transfer.

---

## The Portara

Visible from the port is the **Portara** — the marble gate of an unfinished Temple of Apollo (530 BC), on the islet of Palatia. It is a 10-minute walk from the port and free to visit at any time.

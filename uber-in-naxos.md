# Is Uber Available in Naxos? (2026)

**No — Uber does not operate in Naxos.** This is one of the most common questions tourists ask before arriving.

---

## Why Uber does not work in Naxos

Uber operates in Athens and a small number of Greek cities, but it does not cover the Cyclades islands. There is no rideshare app (Uber, Bolt, FREE NOW) that works on Naxos.

---

## What to use instead

### 1. Pre-booked private transfer (recommended)

A private transfer is the closest equivalent to Uber in terms of convenience — you book in advance, a driver meets you at arrival with a name board, and the price is fixed with no surprises.

Advantages over Uber / metered taxi:
- Fixed price confirmed before travel
- Driver tracks your ferry or flight — if you're delayed, the driver waits
- English-speaking, professional drivers
- Air-conditioned Mercedes vehicles
- Available 24 hours

### 2. Metered taxi

Naxos has a metered taxi fleet available at:
- The port (main taxi rank)
- Chora (Naxos Town) central rank
- By phone — local taxi numbers available at hotels and the port

**Taxi fare basics:**
- Starting fee: €3.50
- Per km: €0.70 (daytime, outside town)
- Night surcharge (24:00–05:00): base fare doubles
- Booking by phone: +€4 surcharge
- Cash only — taxis do not accept cards

Taxis can be hard to find during peak ferry arrivals in summer. Pre-booking is strongly recommended.

---

### 3. KTEL buses

The public bus network covers the main beaches from Chora bus terminal. No app — buy tickets on the bus or at the terminal. Does not reach most hotels and villas.

---

### 4. Car rental

Available at the port and airport. Good option if staying 3+ days and you want full flexibility.

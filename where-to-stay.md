## Chora (Naxos Town)

**Best overall base for first-time visitors.**

- Walking distance to the port, Portara, old town, restaurants, and shops
- Bus connections to the beach strip
- Most hotel and rental variety
- Lively in the evenings without being as loud as Mykonos

Downside: no beach in Chora itself (nearest is Agios Georgios, 1.5 km).

---

## Agios Prokopios

**Best beach base — most popular choice.**

- The best-equipped beach area on the island: sunbeds, water sports, restaurants, bars
- Wide range of hotels, studios, and villas
- Good for families (shallow water) and young travellers (beach bar scene)
- 8 km from the port — transfer needed, buses run frequently in summer

**Transfer from port:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

---

## Agia Anna

**Best for couples and those wanting character.**

- Smaller than Agios Prokopios, more atmosphere
- Small fishing pier, tavernas on the sand, relaxed pace
- Connected by sand to both Agios Prokopios (north) and Plaka (south)

**Transfer from port:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

---

## Plaka

**Best for long stays and those wanting space.**

- 4 km of uncrowded beach with no overdevelopment
- Cedar-backed dunes, fewer sunbeds, more natural feel
- Quieter than Agios Prokopios — better for families with children or those avoiding noise

**Transfer from port:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

---

## Mikri Vigla

**Best for windsurfers and kitesurfers.**

- 200+ windy days per year — one of Europe's top kite/windsurf spots
- Flisvos Kite Centre on the beach with lessons, rental, and accommodation
- Very quiet if wind is not the reason you are here — limited restaurants and nightlife

**Transfer from port:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com)

---

## Practical notes

- **If you arrive late at night:** Pre-book a transfer regardless of where you are staying — taxis are scarce after 22:00
- **If you are visiting inland villages:** Staying in Chora and taking a private day tour is more practical than renting a car and driving mountain roads at night
- **Getting between areas:** Private transfer, taxi, or bus. The bus only covers the main beach strip

# How to Get to Naxos — Ferry, Flight & Transfer Guide (2026)

Naxos is accessible by ferry from Piraeus (Athens) and by direct flight from Athens and several European cities. This guide covers every route and what to do once you arrive.

---

## By ferry from Athens (Piraeus)

The most popular way to reach Naxos. Ferries depart from the Port of Piraeus, about 30 minutes from central Athens by Metro (Line 1 or Line 3).

### Journey times

| Ferry type | Duration | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| High-speed (Seajets, Hellenic Seaways) | 3h 25min – 4h | Faster but rougher in wind |
| Conventional (Blue Star Ferries) | 5h – 6h | More stable, cabins available |

### Frequency

Up to 7 sailings per day in summer. First departure from Piraeus around 06:45, last around 17:30.

### Ticket prices

From €52 economy deck. Cabins and business class available on Blue Star Ferries.

**Book ferry tickets:** ferryhopper.com, directferries.com, or at the port. Book 2–3 months ahead for July–August.

### Tip — book your transfer before you sail

Naxos taxis are scarce when a large Blue Star ferry docks. Pre-booking a private transfer means your driver is waiting regardless of the crowd.

**Pre-book arrival transfer:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com/naxos-port-transfer](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com/naxos-port-transfer)

## By plane

**Naxos National Airport (JNX)** receives domestic flights.

| From | Airline | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Athens (ATH) | Sky Express, Olympic Air | 45 min |
| Thessaloniki | Sky Express (seasonal) | ~1h |
| Rhodes | Sky Express (seasonal) | ~1h |

The airport is 3 km from Chora. There is no bus. A taxi or private transfer is needed.

**Pre-book airport transfer:** [naxiancoasttransfers.com/naxos-airport-transfer](https://naxiancoasttransfers.com/naxos-airport-transfer)

---

## Arriving at the port — what to expect

- The port is in Chora (Naxos Town), the main town
- The Portara (Temple of Apollo gate) is visible immediately as you dock
- Taxis queue at the port exit — supply is limited in peak season
- Your hotel may be within walking distance if you are staying in Chora
- For all other destinations, pre-book a transfer or take a taxi

---

## Arriving at the airport — what to expect

- Single terminal, straightforward
- No bus service to beach villages
- Taxi rank outside arrivals — limited vehicles
- Pre-booked private transfer is the most reliable option

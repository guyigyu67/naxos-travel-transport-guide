# Naxos Food Guide — What to Eat & Where (2026)

Naxos is the most fertile island in the Cyclades and produces ingredients found nowhere else in Greece. This is a serious food destination.

---

## Why Naxos food is different

Naxos is the only Cycladic island that is fully self-sufficient in food. The fertile Tragea plain and the mountain villages produce:
- Cheese (graviera, arseniko, anthotyros)
- Meat (lamb, pork, free-range chicken)
- Naxian potatoes — famous throughout Greece, with a distinct earthy sweetness from the volcanic soil
- Honey (thyme honey from the mountain villages)
- Citron fruit — basis of the island's unique kitron liqueur
- Olive oil, figs, almonds

---

## Must-try local dishes

| Dish | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Graviera** | Naxos's most famous cheese — semi-hard, nutty, golden. Eaten fresh or grilled (saganaki). |
| **Arseniko** | Stronger, aged hard cheese. Often grated over pasta. |
| **Naxian potatoes** | Roasted, fried, or in a salad. Completely different from supermarket potatoes. |
| **Kokoras me makaronia** | Rooster slow-braised in tomato and red wine sauce, served over pasta. |
| **Rosto** | Naxian pork casserole with onions, wine, and spices. A Sunday dish in village homes. |
| **Fresh seafood** | Grilled octopus, sea bream, red mullet at harbour tavernas. Best at Apollonas. |
| **Kitron** | Liqueur made from citron leaves, unique to Naxos. Yellow (sweet), green (medium), clear (dry). Taste it at the Vallindras distillery in Halki. |
| **Thyme honey** | Intensely fragrant, from mountain-grazing bees. Buy a jar to take home. |

## Best places to eat (by area)

### Chora (Naxos Town)
- Extensive choice along the port waterfront and in the old town lanes
- For atmosphere: eat inside the Kastro (old town) where the restaurants are quieter
- Avoid the obvious tourist traps on the main waterfront strip — walk one street back for better quality at lower prices

### Mountain villages
- **Filoti:** Traditional tavernas on the central square — order the lamb and local wine
- **Halki:** Vallindras distillery (kitron tasting, free), small cafe on the square
- **Apeiranthos:** Tavernas with views over the valley — generous portions, authentic recipes

### Apollonas (north coast)
- Best fish lunch on the island — the tavernas are right on the fishing harbour
- Grilled octopus, fresh sea bream, Naxian salad with local cheese
